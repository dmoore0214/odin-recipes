Biscuits and Gravy

Biscuits and Gravy are a staple in many southern homes. This is one of my favorite recipes to make.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Whole Milk
  • Butter or Oil
  • Can of Biscuits

Steps

  1. Bake the Can of Biscuits.
  2. Melt the Butter or Oil for 2 minutes.
  3. Slowly add the flour to the melted butter/oil mixture
  4. Whisk in the milk until everything is smooth.
  5. Cook mixture until it is the thickness preferred.
