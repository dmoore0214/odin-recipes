Eggs In A Basket

This is my favorite way to make eggs!

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Toast
  1. Butter a heated pan.
  2. Cut a whole out of the bread leaving a border around the hole.
  3. Place toast on heated pan and piece you cut out.
  4. When toast is lightly toasted, crack egg in the hole.
  5. Flip toast with egg in it, so all the whites cook.
